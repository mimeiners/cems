Spring-Mass-Damping System#

  • 1-D equation of motion (EoM) \(F = m\ddot{x} + d\dot{x} + kx\)

  • Laplace transformation

\[\begin{split} \begin{align} H(s) &= \frac{1}{m s^2 + d s + k} \\ &= \frac{\frac{1}{m}}{s^2 + \frac{\omega_0}{Q} s + \omega_0^2} \end{align} \end{split}\]

Functional Block Diagram#

  • Coriolis force principle, \(F_C = 2 m \left(\mathbf{v} \times \Omega \right)\)

  • Drive loop to have an accelerated mass

  • Sense loop to detect angular rate

  • Distinction of closed-loop and open-loop system

Multi-Domain Readout Block Diagram#

\[\begin{split} \begin{align} H_s(s) &= \frac{\frac{1}{m}}{s^2 + \frac{\omega_0}{Q}s + \omega_0^2} &\quad \mbox{sensor} \\ H_{CV}(s) &= \frac{g_m}{C_L}\frac{1-e^{-sT_{int}}}{s} &\quad \mbox{CV converter} \\ H_{lf}(z) &= -\frac{z}{z+a}\frac{z^2 +b_1z +b_0}{z^2 +c_1z +c_0} &\quad \mbox{loop filter} \end{align} \end{split}\]

ROM Modelling - Coordinate transformation#

\[\begin{split} \begin{align} M \ddot{x} &+ K x &= F &\quad \mbox{FEM, 100.000 DOF}\\ M \phi \ddot{q} &+ K \phi q &= F & \\ \underbrace{\phi' M \phi} \ddot{q} &+ \underbrace{\phi' K \phi} q &= \phi' F & \\ \tilde{M} \ddot{q} &+ \tilde{K} q &= \tilde{F} & \quad \mbox{ROM, approx. 10 DOF} \end{align} \end{split}\]
